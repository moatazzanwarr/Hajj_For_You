Hajj is the greatest gift for 3 simple reasons:

It repays a debt owed to Allah.

A woman from Juhaynah came to the Prophet (peace be upon him) and said:“My mother vowed to go for Hajj, but she did not go for Hajj before she died. Should I do Hajj on her behalf?” He said:…Pay off the debt that is owed to Allah…” (Al-Bukhari, 1754)

Reward of ‘Hajj is complete forgiveness and paradise.

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: “Whoever performs Hajj to this house – Ka’bah- and does not commit any obscenity and wrongdoing, he/she, will come out as the day he/she was born – pure and free from sins.” [Al Bukhari and Muslim]

Hajj is something most Muslims look forward to throughout their lives.

How painful is it for a person to not have that dream fulfilled? Imagine paying off a debt like ‘hajj and giving that reward to a person in the grave or to a person who is unable to perform ‘hajj! How awesome is that?

We pray that Allah grants you the reward of ‘hajj for showing such generosity towards your loved one(s). Watch the video of Ustadh Nouman explaining why ‘Hajj is the most unique act of worship (‘ibaadah) and is the legacy of our father Ibrahim (AS).

GREATEST GIFT: HAJJ FOR YOU

The fifth pillar of Islam – Hajj, one of the most rewarding acts in Islam, completed annually on behalf of deceased / terminally ill Muslims who did not have the chance to perform it themselves. Each Hajj Badal (حج بدل) is performed by qualified students of knowledge, who meet all the conditions and are supervised by a reputable Imam.

GIVE THE GIFT OF HAJJ

1. CHOOSE GIFT

Choose one of two ultimate gifts: Hajj Badal (حج البدل) or Umrah Badal to be performed by qualified students of knowledge in Makkah.

2. MAKE PAYMENT

Make payment online. Receive confirmation. See FAQ for alternative payment options: bank transfer, Direct deposit, certified cheque etc.

3. WE ARRANGE

Hajj Badal or Umrah Badal is performed on behalf of your loved one(s); the process is overseen by a registered Imam in Makkah.

4. CERTIFICATION

Receive a certification of completion consisting of the student’s name and Imam’s stamp after the Hajj Badal is performed.

OUR PURPOSE

HajjBadal.net is an innovative service set up to achieve 2 simple yet noble goals:

  1. To facilitate the 5th pillar of Islam for those who didn’t get the chance to do Hajj/umrah. Hajj by Us makes it easy for family members with the opportunity to give the ultimate gift to their deceased or permanently ill relatives.
  2. To financially assist students and graduates of Islamic Universities (in Saudi Arabia) with extra money to support their studies & livelihood (buy books, travel for knowledge etc.). All requests of Hajj Badal (بدل حج) & Umrah Badals (بدل عمرة) are performed by qualified students of knowledge.

The Prophet (peace be upon said): “The one who shows/calls another to a good deed, is given the same reward as the one who performs it”. So, it’s a win win situation for everyone and we pray that Allah grants us the reward of all ‘hajj and ‘umrahs completed through this facilitation.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHO PERFORMS HAJJ BADAL & UMRAH BADAL?

Qualified students of knowledge from Madinah University, Umm Al Qurra University in Makkah, or Daar Al Hadeeth in Makkah will be appointed to perform the hajj badal or umrah badal on your behalf.

IS UD’HIYA / SACRIFICE / QURBANI INCLUDED FOR HAJJ BADAL?

Due to recent price increase for Udhiya in Saudi Arabia, we are now making it an optional payment as it’s not required as part of Hajj Ifraad on behalf of a deceased.

I’M NOT FROM CANADA, CAN I STILL REGISTER?

Absolutely! Every Muslim is welcome; despite their age, gender, language, or country! To date we fulfilled requests from USA, Canada, Australia, UK, France, UAE, Malaysia, New Zealand, Saudi Arabia, UAE, India, Bangladesh, and Pakistan. We are here to help you give the ultimate gift to your loved ones – regardless of where you are from?

CAN I MAKE PAYMENTS VIA ALTERNATE METHODS? I DON’T HAVE A CREDIT CARD.

Yes, absolutely. If you are from outside Canada, you can send us the payment through Bank Transfer / Western Union / other means. If you are in Canada, you make direct deposit to our bank account or send us a certified cheque. For more details see FAQ and Contact Us.

CAN I SEND GIFTS / PAY EXTRA FOR THE PERSON PERFORMING ‘HAJJ / ‘UMRAH?

Yes, absolutely. However, we are only able to process monetary gifts at the moment due to logistical reasons. Please contact us should you wish to send special gifts to the person performing ‘Hajj / ‘Umrah for your loved ones.
