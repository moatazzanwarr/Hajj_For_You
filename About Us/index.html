About Us

THE PEOPLE & THEIR PURPOSE

HajjByUS, the organization behind this site, is an innovative service set up to achieve 2 simple but noble goals:

  1. 1- To facilitate the 5th pillar of Islam for those who didn’t get the chance to do Hajj/umrah. Hajj by Us makes it easy for family members / friends with the opportunity to give the ultimate gift to their deceased or terminally ill Muslim men and women. Whether it is someone who cannot do Hajj for health reasons, old age, or it is someone who has passed away, we would like to provide this service to facilitate the fifth pillar of Islam and this beautiful act of worship.
  2. 2- To financially assist students and graduates of Islamic Universities (in Saudi Arabia) with extra money to support their studies & livelihood (buy books, travel for knowledge etc.).
  • So, when you book with us, you are not only benefiting your loved ones, but also investing into sadaqah jaariyah for your loved ones. The Prophet (pbuh) said: “even the fishes in the sea makes du’a for the person seeking knowledge”. Therefore, your support to such individuals opens doors for ajr in perpetuity! Allahu Akbar!
  • The program received a wide support from Muslims from all over the world with requests from countries including USA, Canada, Australia, UK, UAE, Malaysia, New Zealand, Bangladesh, Pakistan.
  • HajjByUs is registered in Toronto, Canada and run by reputable brothers with over a decade of collective experience leading non-profit community programs – locally and globally.

Our Commitments

How painful is it for a person to not have that dream fulfilled? Imagine paying off a debt like ‘hajj and giving that reward to a person in the grave or to a person who is unable to perform ‘hajj! How awesome is that?

1. CHOOSE GIFT

Choose one of two ultimate gifts: Hajj Badal (حج البدل) or Umrah Badal to be performed by qualified students of knowledge in Makkah.

2. MAKE PAYMENT

Make payment online. Receive confirmation. See FAQ for alternative payment options: bank transfer, Direct deposit, certified cheque etc.

3. WE ARRANGE

Hajj Badal or Umrah Badal is performed on behalf of your loved one(s); the process is overseen by a registered Imam in Makkah.

Receive a certification of completion consisting of the student’s name and Imam’s stamp after the Hajj Badal is performed.
